Overview
Our graduate capstone team spent three months researching and designing a global navigation system that spans across multiple business systems and encourages products cross-selling.

The final deliverable was designed in Figma and bridges existing Assurant lines of businesses, creating a cohesive one-stop hub that improves Assurant brand awareness, product retention, and sales. Along with this, we developed a slew of UX artifacts, detailing work that we carried out throughout the UX journey; this includes surveys, interview protocols, affinity maps, personas, user journeys, information architecture, wireframes, and user test insights.
