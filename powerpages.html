Overview

Microsoft Power Pages is a secure, enterprise-grade, low-code software as a service (SaaS) platform for creating, hosting, and administering modern external-facing business websites. 

My role was to focus on Power Pages Data Workspace including “Data” in the Maker studio where users can view, manage and interact with both data and data powered component, providing supports with competitive audit, design solutions, and interative prototypes. Another part of supporting the Design team included learning best accessibility practices, establishing design patterns, and defining accessibility specs for team to use a reference. Collaborate with other PM to help deliver marketing design projects for the multiple Microsoft events (GA/Build/Ignite/Inspire).
