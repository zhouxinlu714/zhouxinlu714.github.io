Overview

Triple C official website is the central branding hub for an organization(Chinese Computer Community) in UCSD. It introduces quarterly projects, releases the newest announcements and showcases gallery.Its user-friendly UI enables visitors to easily browse project details and event timelines. Its built-in content management system helps administrators quickly update website information. Prospective members can also submit their resume and contact forms through the website.

UC San Diego-Chinese Computer Community Official Website

Tools: HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Figma

Skills: Visual Design, Coding

Timeline: 3 months (September - December 2018)

Background

Living in a digital world, a website is the fastest way to increase visibility of an organization. As long as the organization was founded, I worked with a team of PM and web developers and spent a quarter on building this whole website. We believe the website itself can be used to accomplish more than an information presenting platform. We aimed to make this website function as a “window”, through which information about organization, project, members would have no barrier to flow to audiences, reversly, students could also reach out more people focused in similar academic field.

Feature

Home

The main page of Triple C official website integrate multiple information in one page. Here audiences can see brand story about this organization, event timeline in a quarter and photos of each event. Since this organization is a computer science club, I want to include more computer elements in the design. In event timeline page, after clicking on each button on timeline, the detailed description will be show on the right screen in a terminal style.

Member

On the member page, all members in organization were listed because I believe in any company or organization where the people behind are as important as the company or organization itself. It can adds a personal touch to the organization and can lend trust to visitors. Tabs above profiles were used to divide people into each department and it is easier for visitor to look for specific person. In addition, after hovering on each portrait, information icons with links including email, github, and LinkedIn will pop-up.

Project

The project page is mainly to provide short introduction about each individual project. Visitors can see a short description, web/mobile interfaces demo, project timeline, and people have been worked on this project.

Contact

he contact us page enable visitors to contact the organization by filling out the form including email, mobile number and message. Social media icons besides form were also clickable to direct visitors to other pages.

Our Next Steps...
