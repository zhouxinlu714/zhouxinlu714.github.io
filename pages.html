Microsoft Power Pages

Web Design AI design SaaS Platform Accessibility
Overview

Microsoft Power Pages is a secure, enterprise-grade, low-code software as a service (SaaS) platform for creating, hosting, and administering modern external-facing business websites. 

My role was to focus on Power Pages Data Workspace including “Data” in the Maker studio where users can view, manage and interact with both data and data-powered component, providing supports with competitive audit, design solutions, and interative prototypes. Another part of my responsibility is to support the design team to learn best accessibility practices, establish design patterns, and define accessibility specs for team to use a reference. I also collaborated with many PMs to deliver marketing design projects for the multiple Microsoft events (GA/Build/Ignite/Inspire). After the new features were released, the product experienced remarkable growth, as evidenced by a significant increase in its Net Promoter Score (NPS) of 48 and 140% growth in Monthly Active Users (MAU).
